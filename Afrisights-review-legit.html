What is AfriSight?

AfriSight is a premier data collection platform with a strong presence across Africa. By joining the AfriSight community, you can participate in surveys that influence the development of products and services that matter to you.

As a registered member, you'll have the opportunity to share your opinions, complete online surveys, and drive real-time change while earning rewards for your time and insights.

Our platform boasts thousands of members across multiple African countries, making us one of the most extensive survey networks on the continent.

How Does AfriSight Work?

Getting started with AfriSight is simple and only takes a few minutes.

1. Sign Up

Create your free AfriSight account and complete your profile to help us match you with relevant surveys.

2. Complete Surveys

Receive survey invitations via email and share your honest opinions on various topics.

3. Earn Rewards

Get paid for each completed survey. Redeem your earnings via PayPal, airtime, or shopping vouchers.
